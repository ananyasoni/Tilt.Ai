Poker Rules

Basic Rules

Poker is a family of card games that combines gambling, strategy, and skill. The winner is determined by the ranks and combinations of their cards, some of which remain hidden until the end of the game.

Common Hands:

  • High Card
  • One Pair
  • Two Pair
  • Three of a Kind
  • Straight
  • Flush
  • Full House
  • Four of a Kind
  • Straight Flush
  • Royal Flush
